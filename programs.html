Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning experiences

Pre-School Program (3-5 years)

Our pre-school program nurtures young minds through play-based learning and gentle guidance.

  • Sensory exploration and development
  • Creative expression through art, music and movement
  • Social skills and emotional development
  • Language development and pre-literacy activities
  • Foundation for mathematical thinking
  • Nature exploration and environmental awareness

Timings

9:00 AM - 12:30 PM (Half Day)

9:00 AM - 3:00 PM (Full Day)

Teacher-Student Ratio

1:10 for optimal attention

Kindergarten Program (5-6 years)

Our kindergarten program builds upon the foundation of pre-school and prepares children for primary education.

  • Early literacy development and phonics
  • Number sense and basic mathematics
  • Scientific inquiry and exploration
  • Collaborative learning and project work
  • Problem-solving skills and critical thinking
  • Cultural awareness and value education

Timings

9:00 AM - 3:00 PM (Monday - Friday)

Teacher-Student Ratio

1:15 with focused guidance
