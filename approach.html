Our Approach

Discover our unique educational methodology

Integral Education

Our approach is based on Sri Aurobindo's philosophy of integral education, which focuses on the complete development of the child.

Five-fold Development

Our approach nurtures all dimensions of the child

Physical

Development of the body through movement, coordination, and healthy habits.

  • Regular outdoor play
  • Physical activities
  • Motor skill development

Vital

Nurturing emotions, desires, enthusiasm, and energy.

  • Emotional intelligence
  • Creative expression
  • Healthy relationships

Mental

Developing thinking, creativity, curiosity, and clarity.

  • Inquiry-based learning
  • Critical thinking
  • Creative exploration

Psychic

Connecting with inner being—intuition, compassion, and harmony.

  • Quiet reflection
  • Mindfulness practices
  • Inner guidance

Spiritual

Fostering awareness of the divine presence within and all around.

  • Nature connection
  • Gratitude practices
  • Universal values

Our Practice

How we implement Integral Education in daily life

Gentle, Rhythm-Based Routines

Our days flow in a natural rhythm that provides security while allowing flexibility for emergent learning and the needs of the moment.

Curiosity Over Competition

We foster intrinsic motivation and the joy of discovery rather than external rewards or competitive comparisons.

Learning Through Multiple Modalities

Children engage with concepts through play, stories, art, nature, and meaningful experiences that engage all senses.

Mindful Facilitators

Our teachers see themselves as facilitators of learning rather than mere instructors, accompanying each child on their unique journey.
