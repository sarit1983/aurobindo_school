About Us

Get to know our school's philosophy and vision

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment where children can develop holistically - physically, mentally, emotionally, and spiritually - through the principles of Integral Education.

We follow the path laid by Sri Aurobindo and The Mother, who saw education as the awakening of consciousness, not the mere transfer of information.

Our Core Values

Respect

Honoring each child's unique journey and natural rhythm of development. We believe that every child has their own path and pace of learning.

Harmony

Nurturing balanced growth across all aspects of being—physical, vital, mental, psychic, and spiritual—creating inner coherence and joy.

Awareness

Fostering consciousness and mindfulness in daily activities. We encourage children to be present and aware in their learning journey.

Growth

Supporting the natural unfolding of each child's potential. We provide an environment that nurtures continuous growth and self-discovery.
