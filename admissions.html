Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal age to start at Sri Aurobindo Integral School?
We accept children from age 3 onwards. Our pre-school program is designed for children aged 3-5 years, providing a gentle introduction to structured learning through play and exploration.
What is your approach to education?
We follow Sri Aurobindo's Integral Education philosophy, which focuses on the complete development of the child - physical, vital, mental, psychic, and spiritual. Our approach combines traditional academic excellence with holistic development.
What are the class sizes?
We maintain small class sizes to ensure individual attention. Pre-school classes typically have 12-15 children with two teachers, while kindergarten classes have 15-18 children.
What are the school timings?
School hours are from 8:30 AM to 2:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We also offer after-school programs until 4:00 PM for families needing extended care.
